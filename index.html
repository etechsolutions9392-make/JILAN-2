System User Guide & Rules

1. High-Speed Billing & POS

  • Keyboard Ninja Mode: Use Up / Down arrows to navigate search, Enter to add. Press F2 to Checkout and F4 to Hold Cart.
  • PDF Auto-Pricing: Drag any PDF file directly from your computer and drop it anywhere on the billing screen. The customer will see a "Processing" overlay while it calculates!
  • Floating Calculator: Click the "Calculator" button at the top. You can drag it anywhere. When you finish calculating, press the purple "Push" button to instantly drop the result into the Custom Item price box!
  • Voice Commands: Click the microphone icon and say "Add 2 pens" or "Oka lamination". The AI will translate and add it.

2. Khata / Credit System (Pay Later)

  • Giving Credit: Select "Credit (Pay Later)" in the Payment Mode dropdown. Note: You MUST enter a valid 10-digit mobile number so the debt is tracked.
  • Settling Dues: The next time the customer gives you their number in the billing screen, a red alert will appear saying "Pending Due". Click "Settle" to log their payment.

3. Smart Inventory Management

  • Auto-Merging Stock: To prevent duplicate entries, the system uses Smart Match. If you add a product manually or via CSV that has the same Barcode (or the exact same Name + Company combination), it will automatically merge the new stock into the old item and update the pricing!
  • CSV Bulk Upload: Click "Template" to download the exact Excel/CSV format required. Fill it out, click "Import CSV", and thousands of items will upload in 1 second.

4. Audits & WhatsApp Receipts

  • Digital Loyalty Wallet: The WhatsApp receipt link now includes a beautiful Customer Portal where they can see their accumulated points and lifetime savings!
  • Deep Audit Logs: Everything is tracked. The Activity Log tracks logins, stock modifications, sales, and deleted products securely.
  • Blind Cash Tally: When staff close the register, they cannot see the expected cash. They must enter what they actually have. Only the Admin dashboard logs the discrepancy.

Strict System Rules

1. Never share Admin credentials.
2. Managers cannot see actual product costs or net profit.
3. Factory Reset can only be done by Admin typing 'RESET'.

    Quick Troubleshooting

    Buttons not working or Data missing?

    Solution: Ensure you haven't accidentally modified critical HTML IDs. Check your internet connection to sync cloud data. If the issue persists, clear your browser cache.

    Receipt Printer alignment issues?

    Solution: Open the browser print dialog. Set Paper Size to 'Thermal 80mm' or 'A4', disable 'Headers & Footers', and set Margins to 'None'.
